WHAT IS FACIAL SPA?

HerbaLine Facial Spa combines professional beauty techniques with trendy innovative spa treatments. Its modern contemporary surroundings and high quality services are aimed at providing each customer relief from the stresses of both the mind and the body. Products of complete natural ingredients, a natural “Zen” environment, professional facial consultants and innovative techniques at every HerbaLine outlet all underline our commitment to our business philosophy of providing “Stress-Free Treatments”.

COMMUNITY CONCEPT

HerbaLine has 28 branches and more than 27 franchise outlets, located in central communal locations across Malaysia and Brunei. Special care is taken in choosing each location, to ensure convenience and ease for our customers.
